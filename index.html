Create Profile

Job Seeker

Create Profile

Provide your details and skills based on which you want to find right onsite/remote job for you

Job Provider

Create a Job requirement with skill details to find right Job Seeker match. You can attach a job description document for details.

Job Seeker

Review and submit proposal for open jobs based on your skills and rating received for previous completed jobs on platform. Option to propose your duration and price for the job.

Job Seeker and Job Provider

Both parties can chat in APP to finalize and answer queries. This builds mutual trust and understanding for job execution before entering in the deal

Job Provider

Confirm the proposal and deal is locked by holding the job contract amount in secured wallet

Job Seeker

Start and complete the job

Job Provider

Marks job as completed and instantly release the job payment amount to Seeker Wallet and gives performance rating. Rating helps Job seeker to secure more and more work on the platform.

Job Seeker

Confirm payment receipt and after giving rating to provider

Initiate withdraw money to bank account.
